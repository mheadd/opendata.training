Got Data?

When it comes to releasing open data, there is simply no substitute for experience.

Increasingly, state and local governments are under legislative or administrative mandates to release data to the public. Some governments are evaluating the release of open data as part of broader transparency, economic development or innovation efforts.

Open Data Training is a training seminar and workshop designed specifically for people working in state and local governments. It is meant for those who are interested in releasing open data or just looking to learn more about what governments are doing with data, what benefits they are realizing and - most importantly - what challenges they are facing.
